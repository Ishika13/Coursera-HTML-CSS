BUTTERBEER SLUSH RECEPIE

Ingredients to make Slush

  • Ice cubes about 10-12 big pieces
  • 1 Tbsp. Kosher salt table salt is fine, too
  • 1 cup cream soda
  • 1 tsp. butterscotch ice cream topping

Ingredients for Cream

  • 1 c. heavy cream or whipping cream
  • 1/4 c. butterscotch topping

Instructions for Butterbeer

  1. Place cream soda and butterscotch in a Ziploc bag, press out all the air and seal tightly. Mix it until the butterscotch blends with the cream soda.
  2. Place bag of soda in the bag of ice and shake for about 3-5 minutes or until the soda slushy.
  3. Beat the cream and butterscotch with a mixer on high speed and pour slush into a cup topped with butterscotch cream.
