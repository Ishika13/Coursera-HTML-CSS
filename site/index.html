Butterbeer Slush Recepie

Ingredients to make Slush

  • Ice cubes about 10-12 big pieces
  • 1 Tbsp. Kosher salt table salt is fine, too
  • 1 cup cream soda
  • 1 tsp. butterscotch ice cream topping

Ingredients for Cream

  • 1 c. heavy cream or whipping cream
  • 1/4 c. butterscotch topping

Instructions for Butterbeer

  1. Place cream soda and butterscotch in a sandwich-sized Ziploc bag, press out all the air and seal tightly. Mix around until the butterscotch is mixed in with the cream soda.
  2. Place bag of soda in the bag of ice and seal.Shake for about 3-5 minutes or until the soda is nice and slushy.
  3. Beat the cream and butterscotch with a mixer on high speed until stiff peaks form. Pour slush into a cup and top with butterscotch cream topping.
